## FAQ

**How do I add items?**
Type any item in the text field at the bottom of the screen. Pickd will automatically categorize it for you.

**What is Store Mode?**
Store Mode reorganizes your list by store aisle so you can shop efficiently. Tap the cart icon to activate it.

**How do I add items with Siri?**
Say "Add [item] to my Pickd list" and it will be added automatically.

**How do I manage multiple lists?**
Multiple lists are available with Pickd Premium. Tap the sparkle icon to learn more.

**How do I restore my purchase?**
Open the upgrade screen and tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom.

**What languages are supported?**
English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, and Portuguese (Brazil).

## Privacy

Pickd does not collect or share any personal data. Your grocery lists are stored locally on your device and optionally synced via iCloud.
