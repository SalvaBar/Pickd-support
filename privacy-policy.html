Privacy Policy for Pickd

Last updated: April 5, 2026

Overview

Pickd is a grocery list app. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. All your grocery lists and data are stored locally on your device.

Third-Party Advertising

The free version of Pickd displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain data for ad personalization, including device identifiers, coarse location (based on IP address), and usage data. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

Pickd offers a one-time premium purchase to remove ads. Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple and are subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data (beyond what AdMob collects via IP)
  • Photos, health data, or financial information
  • Account credentials or passwords

Children's Privacy

Pickd does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
